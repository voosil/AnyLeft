AnyLeft — 图标方案

Six macOS app-icon directions for the LLM-quota tracker, all built on the warm-modernism palette and the ◑ "how much is left" motif. Each shown at large and dock-small size. 六个方向，均取「剩多少」的表盘意象。

1a Half gauge · 半月表

The literal menu-bar ◑ glyph, enlarged. The single clearest reading of "remaining."

Gold progress ring on navy, with a reset-tick at top. Echoes the cabinet-card gauges.

A needle sweeping a ticked arc — the "Ration" fuel-gauge metaphor made literal.

1d Meter bars · 用量条

Four provider-colored bars on dark. Speaks to "many plans at a glance" over a single gauge.

1e Battery · 余量电池

Universally-understood "charge left." Warmest, friendliest read of the six.

1f 剩 glyph · 汉字标

The "剩" of 剩了么 as a typographic mark, with a navy status dot. Distinctive; leans on the CN name.
